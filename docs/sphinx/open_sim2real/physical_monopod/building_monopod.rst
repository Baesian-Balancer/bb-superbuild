Building The Monopod
====================

This section hosts all the information required to completely reproduce our
physical platform. This includes; hardware and electrical build instructions and
testing instructions, software installation and setup, and further reading.

.. warning::

  It is highly recommended to read the entirety of this section prior to building
  the platform. In addition to instructions on replication this section
  focuses on the skills and tools and other technical requirements.

If you are interested in reproducing our platform after reading through the documentation
but have any questions or doubts about the platform. Feel free to contact :ref:`the team <the_team>`.
We are invested in seeing this project succeed and appreciate any support, advice or recommendations.
